## Regulatory Environment
The regulatory environment for biosimilars in India is overseen by the Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (CDSCO). The CDSCO has established guidelines for the approval of biosimilars, which include requirements for quality, safety, and efficacy comparability to reference biologics. The guidelines were updated in 2025 to emphasize reduced animal studies and advanced analytical tools.
